SIP of Kota Pioneers

Kota Pioneers offers School Integrated Learning Programs for both Foundation Batch (NTSE & Olympiads) and for class 11th and 12th (Main+Advanced NEET-UG) within the School Campus. It delivers quality education with cutting-edge technological support with combination of one to one interactive classes and comparative study material.

We aim to collaborate with Progressive Schools across the country by setting up the new age Hi-Tech classrooms designed to train students for academic and competitive success where students will avail the benefit of getting tutored in a secured and comfortable environment within the school premises.
